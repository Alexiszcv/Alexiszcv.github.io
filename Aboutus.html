ABOUT US

Hello !

I'm Alexis and I've lived in Paris since I was born, so I've been walking the streets of the capital for 22 years and know every nook and cranny, including some fascinating places that most tourists ignore when they come to Paris.

As I'm passionate about photography, I decided to put my knowledge of the city at the service of visitors, so that you can leave with an unforgettable souvenir, whether you're an influencer, a family or a couple on a romantic trip.
